About me

I am a mobile developer, embedded systems enthusiast and now working into getting better in the fields of Networking and cybersecurity. I have explored Cloud computing and Artificial Intelligence to some level.

The hallmark of my time in the university I can say as to be part of the Nakuja Project which is a student’s-based project here at J.K.U.A.T in collaboration with Africa-ai-Japan and PAUSTI, that is involved in the design and complete fabrication of a rocket. I am delighted to say that I have been part of the airframe team which potentially deals with designing and fabrication of the airframe (body of the rocket) and we are soon going to launch our Nakuja N3 rocket.

Outside school I enjoy a lot of family time, travel, reading, singing, cooking, swimming, wood work, community work especially weekend training in high-schools and motivational talks to students. This project was birth by me and my two team mates Rodney and Kisio and it has been the highlight of my year this far. As the co-founder, I aim and keep striving to see that this product will go to the market and help patients both in the country as well as globally.

I enjoy and aspire in working towards innovative and impactful projects. I love order, consistency and a strong drive to see things through completion.
